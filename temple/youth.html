Philadelphia Temple Youth Cultural Celebration

Information for the Princeton 1st Ward

It was recently announced that our stake will be part of the new Philadelphia temple district! As a part of that, the youth of all the stakes in the temple district have the opportunity to participate in a youth cultural celebration. Practices will begin in mid-July with most of them being just at our stake level. The day of the performance will be September 17, which will be a full day of rehearsal with a performance in the evening. There will be more information coming, but keep this exciting opportunity in mind!

All youth in the Philadelphia Temple District have been invited to participate in the Youth Cultural Celebration on Saturday, September 17. (This includes all who are currently in the youth program as well as those who will turn 12 by September 17.)

July 4 Deadline: Youth Must Provide Costume Measurements in Order to Register to Participate in the Youth Cultural Celebration

  • We need to get a firm count of the number of youth who plan to participate in the Celebration, and we are also required to provide detailed measurements of all participating youth for costumes and t-shirts. We have attached active PDF documents for the youth to fill in their name, unit, and measurements (separate forms for YW and YM). You may choose to have the youth measure themselves at a youth activity, or you may decide to have families handle it at home.
  • The measurement form will double as the official registration form for the youth. Completed forms should be emailed to Isabel Constantin: isabel.constantin@gmail.com. If you opt to fill out hard copies, please mail them to Catherine Foley, 29 Hawthorne Dr., Princeton Junction, NJ 08550.

You may know that President Sikahema was instrumental in getting Philadelphia City approval for the temple to be built there. He also played football for the Philadelphia Eagles and is currently a Philadelphia news anchor.
    • 10:00 a.m.-Noon: Fireside
    • Noon-12:45: Lunch*
    • 12:45-2:45: Dance/music rehearsal
    • 2:45-3:15: Planning meeting for any youth interested in helping with the Celebration banner or the Around-Philadelphia presentation
  • *Lunch will be provided for the youth and for any ward/branch youth leaders who will be staying for the Cultural Celebration dance rehearsal. The youth should wear comfortable, modest clothing and shoes (keeping in mind that we will be in the chapel and then rehearsing afterwards).
